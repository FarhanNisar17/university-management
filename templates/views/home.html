About the University

The University of Kashmir, North Campus, located in the scenic town of Delina, Baramulla, is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation. It provides students with a holistic learning experience, combining modern facilities with a serene academic environment.

Established as part of the University of Kashmir, the North Campus aims to expand higher education opportunities for the youth of north Kashmir. The campus offers programs in Computer Science, Management, Humanities, and other disciplines.
